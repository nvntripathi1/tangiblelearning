Course Curriculum

  • Unit 1: Limits and Continuity 7 hours

    Understanding behavior of functions near specific values.

  • Unit 2: Differentiation Basics 12 hours

    Definition, rules, and applications of derivatives.

  • Unit 3: Applications of Derivatives 12 hours

    Curve sketching, motion, optimization, and rates.

  • Unit 4: Integration 14 hours

    Antiderivatives, definite integrals, and area under curves.
