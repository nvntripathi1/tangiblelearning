Course Overview

Our IB Mathematics AI HL course is designed for students who are fascinated by the power of mathematics to solve complex real-world problems. This comprehensive year-long course combines advanced mathematical techniques with sophisticated applications in statistics, data science, and mathematical modeling. Mathematics AI HL is perfect for students planning careers in data science, economics, psychology, business analytics, or research fields requiring advanced statistical knowledge.

IB Mathematics AI HL provides the most advanced treatment of applied mathematics in the IB programme, emphasizing statistical reasoning, mathematical modeling, and technology-enhanced problem solving. The course covers advanced statistics, probability distributions, hypothesis testing, calculus applications, and sophisticated data analysis techniques essential for university-level research and professional data work.

Higher Level Advantages

Mathematics AI HL provides intensive training in advanced statistics, data analysis, and mathematical modeling. HL students develop sophisticated analytical skills highly valued in data science, research, economics, and advanced business applications.

Detailed Course Curriculum

  • Topic 1: Number and Algebra 23 hours

    Advanced number systems, algebraic techniques, and mathematical modeling.

    • Advanced number systems and approximations
    • Complex algebraic manipulation and modeling
    • Advanced sequences and series with applications
    • Financial mathematics and compound growth models
    • Exponential and logarithmic modeling
    • Advanced equation solving and optimization

  • Topic 2: Functions 31 hours

    Advanced function analysis with sophisticated modeling applications.

    • Advanced function composition and transformations
    • Rational functions and asymptotic behavior
    • Advanced exponential and logarithmic modeling
    • Trigonometric functions and harmonic analysis
    • Piecewise functions and advanced modeling
    • Function optimization and advanced applications

  • Topic 3: Geometry and Trigonometry 25 hours

    Advanced geometric analysis and trigonometric applications.

    • Advanced 2D and 3D geometric analysis
    • Complex trigonometric identities and equations
    • Advanced applications of sine and cosine rules
    • Coordinate geometry and advanced transformations
    • Vector geometry and spatial analysis
    • Advanced area, volume, and optimization problems

  • Topic 4: Statistics and Probability 54 hours

    Advanced statistical analysis, probability theory, and data science techniques.

    • Advanced descriptive statistics and data visualization
    • Sophisticated correlation and regression analysis
    • Advanced probability theory and conditional probability
    • Multiple probability distributions and their applications
    • Advanced hypothesis testing and confidence intervals
    • Chi-squared tests and goodness of fit
    • Time series analysis and forecasting
    • Advanced statistical modeling and interpretation

  • Topic 5: Calculus 28 hours

    Advanced calculus concepts with sophisticated applications.

    • Advanced limits, continuity, and differentiability
    • Complex differentiation techniques and applications
    • Advanced optimization and related rates problems
    • Sophisticated integration techniques and applications
    • Differential equations and mathematical modeling
    • Advanced applications in economics and sciences

  • Mathematical Exploration Preparation

    Comprehensive support for your advanced mathematical investigation.

    • Advanced research question development and methodology
    • Sophisticated data collection and statistical analysis
    • Advanced mathematical modeling and interpretation
    • Professional use of statistical software and technology
    • Advanced mathematical communication and presentation
