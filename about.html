My Story

Welcome to Tangible Learning! I'm Naveen Tripathi, a passionate physics and mathematics educator with 6 years of dedicated teaching experience, specializing in making complex concepts accessible and engaging for students worldwide.

I completed my Bachelor of Technology in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from National Institute of Technology, Warangal, where I developed a strong foundation in electromagnetic theory, circuit analysis, and semiconductor physics.

My journey in education has been incredibly rewarding, having worked with prestigious institutions like AP Guru, Tutopedia, Avanti Learning Centres, and T.I.M.E. (Triumphant Institute of Management Education), as well as online tutoring platforms like TeacherOn.com. Through these experiences, I've developed expertise in both AP and IB curricula, along with advanced understanding of A and AS Level Physics and Maths.

What drives me as an educator is witnessing that "aha!" moment when a challenging concept finally clicks for a student. My background spans from intensive one-on-one tutoring to large group instruction, giving me a comprehensive understanding of diverse learning styles and needs.

I believe that physics and mathematics are not just subjects to be memorized, but fascinating ways to understand our world. My approach focuses on building strong conceptual foundations while ensuring students excel in their examinations.

My Teaching Approach

🔍

Conceptual Understanding

I focus on building a strong foundation by ensuring students truly understand the underlying concepts rather than just memorizing formulas. This approach enables students to tackle novel problems with confidence, even those they haven't seen before.

🧩

Personalized Learning

Every student learns differently. I adapt my teaching methods to match your learning style, strengths, and areas for improvement. This personalized approach ensures that you receive the specific support you need to excel.

📝

Exam Strategies

Beyond content knowledge, I teach specific exam techniques and strategies for AP and IB assessments. Students learn how to approach different question types, manage time effectively, and maximize their scores.

🔄

Regular Assessment

I provide continuous feedback and regular practice assessments to track progress and identify areas that need additional focus. This approach ensures that no concept goes unmastered before moving forward.
